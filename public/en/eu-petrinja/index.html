PROJECT NAME:

Improving the Competitiveness of Geometricus d.o.o. by Investing in the Equipment of a Branch in the City of Petrinja

BENEFICIARY:

Geometricus d.o.o., Ferovac 32, 34340 Kutjevo

DESCRIPTION:

The general aim of the project is to contribute to the economic regeneration of the deprived area of the City of Petrinja through investment in the presence of highly specialized surveying activities in the city.
The specific objective of the project is to increase the competitiveness of the company GEOMETRICUS d.o.o. through the implementation of new technologies and the establishment of a new surveying branch in Petrinja.
This objective will be achieved by raising the level of quality and speed of surveying elaborates, as well as introducing a modern way of processing and providing surveying services, and expanding the portfolio of activities to those needed in the targeted area of the city of Petrinja, especially technologies applicable in natural disaster management.

Results:
- Procured and implemented a new set of surveying instruments based on the latest technologies of Industry 4.0
- Opened and equipped a new surveying branch in the City of Petrinja
- Created 4 new jobs in the year m+2
